Reading Analog Input

I used a potentiometer for this assignment. A potentiometer is a voltage divider, and it allows me to set a variable resistance by turning its knob. When I connected it to the analog pin "A0", the pin sent serial communications to my computer, and I was able to read the voltage on my computer's serial monitor.

Motor Circuit

After building the potentiometer circuit, I adapted it for a DC Motor. A DC Motor directly converts electrical energy into mechanical energy, and is very simple to use. It has a positive and negative side, and it will run as long as there is a powerful enough current going through it.
