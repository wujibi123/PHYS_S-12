Process

I first created a folder where I would store all my components. Each component is in its own separate file. Then I measured the dimensions of each component with a caliiper, storing them as parameters. Here is a screenshot of my parameters for my bearing:

After I measured my parameters, I sketched the basic geometry of each component and extruded them into 3D. I used threading for the nut and screw, and I used joints for the bearing (including decal for aesthetics)
